Business Analytics Guiding Principles

  1. Data transformation should be performed as close to the source as possible, and as close to the user as necessary.
  2. Process is king. Technology change that does not enable process improvement does not move the organization forward.
  3. Dashboards, metrics, and KPIs that are not actionable exist only to serve the vanity of business leaders.
  4. Data science = experimentation; data technology = data science operationalized for business value delivery.
  5. Effective data and analytics leadership are skilled at closing the gap between strategy and execution.
